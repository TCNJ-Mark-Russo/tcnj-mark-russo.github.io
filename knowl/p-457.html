The distanceBetween(...) method declares several variables that are used within that method only, including term1, term2, and distance. These variables have method scope because they are declared within the distanceBetween(...) method and they are destroyed when the method returns. They cannot be accessed from outside the method after its exits because they do not exist at that point.
