double.

  • 64 bits (8 bytes) in size.
  • Can represent numbers with a magnitude ranging from approximately ±4.9 x 10^-324 to ±1.8 x 10^308.
  • Has a precision of about 15 decimal digits.
  • Double literals do not require any suffix but can be suffixed with the letter d for clarity, such as 3.14 or 3.14d.
