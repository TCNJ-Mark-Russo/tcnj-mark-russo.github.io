Listing 11.4.3 is an alternative version of Listing 11.4.1. The definition of printFile(…) in Listing 11.4.3 had no try-catch. Instead, it declares that it throws the checked FileNotFoundException by adding throws FileNotFoundException to the method declaration. This requires us to move the try-catch to main(…) where printFile(…) is invoked. The invocation of this method must be placed in the try-block of the try-catch otherwise the compiler will print an error and fail.
