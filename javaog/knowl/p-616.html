The makeStar(…) method in Listing 7.6.3 builds and returns a star-shaped polygon, progammatically, using the technique described above. The method takes five parameters: (1) npoints, the number of points in the star; (2) inrad, the radius of the inner circle; (3) outrad, the radius of the outer circle; (4) xoffset, the offset of the star location in the x-direction; and (5) yoffset, the offset of the star location in the y-direction.
