A wonderful tool for visualizing the call stack in action is the Java Visualizer 1 , one instance of which is hosted by the University of Waterloo Centre for Education in Mathematics and Computing. You can paste a small Java program into the editor of this tool and watch it execute, step-by-step. The current state of the call stack is illustrated on the right as you step through the program animation. Figure 12.2.3 below is an embedded version of the visualizer for the LiftOff.java program. Scroll to the right to see the call stack. Drag left the right edge of the editor window to bring both into view. Click the [Forward] button to step through the program. Alternatively, click here 2  to visualize the program on the site, directly.
